Research

My long-term goal is to build embodied generalist agents that can acquire skills continually and adaptively and compose the learned skills to solve complicated long-horizon tasks. Towards this goal, I'm doing research at the intersection of robotics, computer vision, and machine learning. Recently, I'm interested in (humanoid) manipulation, learning from humans, and robot foundation models.
