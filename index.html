Research

My long-term goal is to build embodied generalist agents that can leverage predictive world models to acquire skills continually and adaptively and compose the learned skills to solve complicated long-horizon tasks. Towards this goal, I'm doing research at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.
